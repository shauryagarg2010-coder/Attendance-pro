Privacy Policy
Effective: June 2025 · MoneyMate v4.1

1. Data Storage

All your financial data — transactions, goals, bills, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage. MoneyMate does not transmit, upload, or share any of your personal financial data with any server or third party.

2. No Account Required

MoneyMate works fully offline without creating an account. Your profile name and photo are stored locally on your device only.

3. Data You Control

  • You can export your full data as JSON or CSV at any time from the Profile tab.
  • You can delete all your data permanently using "Clear All Data."
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

4. Permissions

MoneyMate may request access to your camera or photo gallery solely to let you set a profile picture. This image is stored locally on your device only.

5. Analytics

MoneyMate does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or any identifiable information.

6. Children's Privacy

MoneyMate is suitable for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@moneymate.app
